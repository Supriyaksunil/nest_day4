The KMCT Group of Institutions is one of the largest groups of educational institutions in Kerala. The objective of KMCT is to impart quality education to aspiring students in different professional fields of their choice. KMCT Group of Educational Institutions focuses on professional development thereby imparting an opportunity for the students to learn and apply new knowledge and skills that will enhance their personal as well as professional life.

CHAIRMAN'S MESSAGE

As we complete 21 years in 2022, I look back with pride at what we have achieved. I would like to thank our students-past and present, parents, our recruiters, and our staff for having stood by us. I am reminded of a lovely quote by Emily Dickinson: “We turn not older with years but newer every day.” Technology has profoundly changed education. Technology enables newer and more innovative means of communication and collaboration. We realize the compelling need to stay abreast with these changes. KMCT is metamorphosing. Rather than resisting change, we have decided to embrace it and move along in the changed context. I welcome you to KMCT- come, be a part of this change.
